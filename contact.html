Frequently Asked

How do I delete my quiz?
Go to My Quizzes, find your quiz by PIN, and delete it. Lost your PIN? Contact us with your quiz title and creation date.
Can I report an inappropriate quiz?
Yes — use the form above with "Content Removal Request" and include the quiz PIN or link. We review all reports within 24 hours.
How long until you reply?
Usually within 24–48 hours. We're a small team but we read every single message.
Open to collabs or features?
Absolutely! Choose "Collab / Partnership" above and tell us about your idea.
