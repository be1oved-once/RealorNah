Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Here's exactly what we do (and don't do) with your data.

Last updated: April 8, 2025

Overview

RealorNah ("we", "our", "us") operates at realornah.vercel.app. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Short version: We collect minimal data to make the app work. We never sell your data. No ads based on your info. Quiz scores belong to you.

What We Collect

When you create a quiz:

  • Quiz questions & answers (stored in Firebase)
  • Your display name (optional)
  • A unique quiz PIN
  • Timestamp of creation

When you take a quiz:

  • Your responses, nickname, score, and result tier
  • Timestamp of attempt

When you contact us:

  • Your name, email, and message

Automatically:

  • Browser/device type, country-level location, pages visited (Vercel Analytics)

How We Use It

  • Store and retrieve your quizzes via Firebase
  • Display leaderboards and results
  • Show trending quizzes (no personal info shown)
  • Respond to contact form messages
  • Send optional email notifications (MailerLite)
  • Improve app performance

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — database & storage
  • Vercel — web hosting & serverless functions
  • Resend — contact form email delivery
  • MailerLite — optional quiz notification emails
  • Google Fonts & Iconify — fonts and icons

Cookies & Local Storage

No advertising or tracking cookies. We use browser localStorage to remember your quiz PIN and save draft data locally. This data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

RealorNah is for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Contact us at contact.globalratings@gmail.com to request deletion.

Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your quiz data
  • Unsubscribe from emails (link in every email)
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Contact us to correct inaccurate info

Email: contact.globalratings@gmail.com or use our Contact page.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes. Continued use = acceptance.

Contact
