How to Use

Everything you need to expose your fake friends

Think your friends really know you?
Let's find out — it takes 2 minutes.

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Create Your Quiz

Tap Create New Quiz from the menu or home page. Enter your name, then add up to 10 questions about yourself — your favourite food, music, weekend vibes, habits, and more. No account needed.

Be honest — make it tricky for your friends!
2
Get Your Unique Link

Once your quiz is ready, you get a unique quiz link that's only yours. Copy it with one tap, or scan the QR code. You can also share directly to WhatsApp or Telegram from the popup.

Tap the copy button — done in one tap!
3
Share With Friends

Send your quiz link via WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, Snapchat, Discord — anywhere you hang out. The more friends take it, the more drama on the leaderboard.

4
Friends Take the Quiz

Your friends open the link, enter their name, and answer all your questions — trying to guess your answers. Each question is multiple choice. Fast, fun, and brutally revealing.

Takes about 2 minutes to complete!
5
See the Leaderboard

Go to My Quiz Stats to see everyone's scores ranked top to bottom. The leaderboard updates live as friends play. Who scored 90%? Who scored 10%? The truth is out.

Low scorers = fake friends confirmed.

New Features

Per-Question Timer New

Enable a countdown timer (10–60 seconds per question) when creating your quiz. Friends must answer before the clock runs out — adds real pressure and catches out anyone who's guessing. Toggle it on/off in the quiz creator.

Icon / Image Mode New

Switch any question from Text Only to With Icons mode. Instead of plain text options, your friends see visual icons (pizza, mountains, gaming controller, etc.) making it more fun and scrollable. Tap the icon picker to choose from 70+ icons across 7 categories.

Quiz Templates New

Don't know what to ask? Browse the Templates section — pick from pre-built quiz packs like College Life, Childhood Friends, Ultimate Bestie, and Couple Quiz. One tap loads all the questions into your editor, ready to customise.

PIN Sync — Access Across Devices New

Your quizzes are saved to your browser by default. With PIN Sync, tap the key icon in the nav bar to generate a 6-digit PIN. Enter the same PIN on any other device and your quiz list syncs instantly — no account, no password, no hassle.

Smart Suggest New

Hit the Suggest button in the quiz creator to instantly auto-fill 3 starter questions. The system detects whether you're in text or icon mode and fills matching questions. Edit them however you want after.

Scorecard Share New

After completing a quiz, tap Share My Score to get a beautifully designed scorecard image with your percentage, creator's name, and your Bestie Tier. Share it to WhatsApp, Telegram, or download it to post anywhere.

Bestie Tiers Explained

Every player gets a Bestie Tier badge based on their final score. Here's what each tier means:

Soulmate
90–100% — You know everything. Suspicious?
Best Friend Forever
71–89% — Real one. The group chat MVP.
Good Friend
51–70% — Decent. Could know you better though.
Suspicious Friend
31–50% — We need to have a talk.
Fake Friend Detected
0–30% — Blocked. Unfollowed. Reported.

Pro Tips

Make tricky questions

Don't make it too obvious. Pick questions where even close friends might guess wrong. The more surprising the results, the more the drama — and the more they'll share it.

Send to as many people as possible

The more people who take your quiz, the better your leaderboard looks. Challenge your whole group chat and see who truly knows you best — the results always surprise.

Use the timer for extra drama

Enable a 15–20 second timer to stop friends from Googling or asking others. When the clock is ticking, real knowledge shows — and fake friends panic.

Create multiple quizzes

Make different quizzes for different friend groups — one for school, one for your close circle, one for family. Manage them all from the My Quizzes dashboard.
