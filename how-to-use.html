How to Use

Everything you need to expose your fake friends 👀

Think your friends really know you?
Let's find out in 3 easy steps! 🔥

Step-by-Step Guide

1
Create Your Quiz

Tap "Create New Quiz" from the menu or home page. Answer up to 10 fun questions about yourself — your favorite food, music, weekend vibes, and more. No account needed!

Tip: Be honest — make it tricky for your friends!
2
Get Your Unique Link

Once your quiz is ready, you'll get a unique link that's only yours. Copy it with one tap and you're ready to share it anywhere.

Just tap the copy button — done!
3
Share With Friends

Send your quiz link to friends via WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, Snapchat, Discord — anywhere you hang out online. The more friends, the more drama! 🔥

4
Friends Take the Quiz

Your friends open the link and answer the same questions — trying to guess your answers. Each question is multiple choice, so it's fast and fun.

Takes about 2 minutes to complete!
5
See the Leaderboard

Head to "My Quiz Stats" to see everyone's scores on your quiz. The leaderboard ranks your friends from real bestie to total stranger. Who scored 90%? Who flopped at 10%? 💀

Low scorers = fake friends confirmed!

Pro Tips

Make tricky questions

Don't make it too obvious! Pick questions where even close friends might guess wrong. The more surprising, the more fun the results.

Send to as many people as possible

The more people who take your quiz, the better your leaderboard looks. Challenge your whole group chat and see who truly knows you best!

Check your stats regularly

Visit "My Quiz Stats" to see new scores as friends complete your quiz. Rankings update in real-time — the drama unfolds live!

Create multiple quizzes

Make different quizzes for different friend groups — one for school, one for your close circle, one for family. Manage them all from your dashboard.
