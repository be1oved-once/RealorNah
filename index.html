FAQ

Is it free?
Yes! 100% free. No login, no credit card, no hidden charges. Just create and share.
Do I need an account?
Nope. Just open, create, share. Your quizzes are saved in your browser automatically.
How many questions?
Up to 10 questions per quiz. Enough to separate the real ones from the fakes.
Can I share on WhatsApp?
Yes! One tap to share on WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, or copy the link anywhere.
Can I see who scored what?
Yes! Every quiz has a live leaderboard showing all your friends' scores ranked top to bottom.
Is there a timer?
Yes! You can enable a per-question timer (10–60 seconds) when creating your quiz to add extra pressure.
What is the PIN sync feature?
PIN sync lets you access your quizzes on any device by entering a 6-digit PIN — no login needed.
