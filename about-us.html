About RealorNah

A viral quiz platform born from fake friends, group chats, and one very petty idea

RealorNah is not a startup built in a boardroom with pitch decks and investor meetings. It's a quiz app built by someone who got tired of friends claiming they "know everything" about you - and then being proven hilariously wrong. No funding. No team. Just one idea: let the quiz do the talking.

50K+ Quizzes Created

The Problem We Actually Faced

It started with a conversation that probably sounds familiar. "I know you better than anyone." Cool. What's my favourite food? "Um… pizza?" Wrong. What music do I actually listen to? "Oh, like… pop?" Dead wrong. What do I do when I'm stressed? Silence.

People assume closeness without actually paying attention. The result? Fake friends who feel real - until you put them to the test.

We had no way to prove who actually paid attention. So we built one.

RealorNah Founder

What RealorNah Actually Does

RealorNah turns your personality, habits, and preferences into a shareable quiz - then lets your friends try to answer it. Every wrong answer is a data point. Every low score is a verdict. It's fun, it's chaotic, and it's brutally honest.

The Core Philosophy

Friendship isn't about time spent together. It's about attention paid. RealorNah measures attention - in quiz form.

  • Create up to 10 personal questions about yourself - habits, favourites, opinions, quirks
  • Get a unique shareable link - no login required for friends who take it
  • Share on WhatsApp, Instagram, Telegram, Discord, Snapchat - anywhere
  • See a live ranked leaderboard - from bestie to total stranger
  • Low scorers are officially exposed as Fake Friends™

Why RealorNah Is Different

Most quiz apps are generic. They give you random personality tests or trivia that has nothing to do with the people in your life. RealorNah is personal. It's about you - and whether the people around you actually know you.

Mission: Make Friendship Honest

The mission is simple: give people a fun, low-pressure way to find out who actually pays attention in their lives. Not to cancel friends. Not to cause drama (okay, maybe a little drama). But to celebrate the ones who genuinely know you - and laugh at the ones who don't.

  • Celebrate the friends who actually know you inside out
  • Turn awkward friendship revelations into something fun and shareable
  • Keep it free - always - because real friendships don't cost money
  • Work perfectly on mobile, because that's where your friends are

You don't need 500 friends. You need 5 who can actually pass the quiz. Quality over quantity - the leaderboard will prove it.
