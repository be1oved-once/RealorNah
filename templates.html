What are friendship quiz templates?

Templates are pre-written sets of quiz questions designed for different friendship types. Instead of starting from scratch, you pick a template — like the Ultimate Bestie Challenge or the College Life Quiz — and customise the answers to match your own life.

They're perfect for sharing on WhatsApp groups, Instagram Stories, or Telegram. Each quiz generates a unique link that your friends can take to test how well they know you. Results show up on a live leaderboard with Bestie Tiers — from Soulmate 👑 to Fake Friend Detected 💀.
